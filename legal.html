Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

MakertMyn (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Delivery Information: Physical address, delivery instructions
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, billing address (processed securely through our payment partners)
  • Communication: Customer service inquiries, reviews, feedback

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, products viewed, search queries
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy below for details

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders
  • Communicating with you about your orders and account
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving our website and services
  • Sending marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing (POPIA Compliance)

We process your personal information based on:

  • Consent: When you provide explicit consent (e.g., marketing emails)
  • Contract Performance: To fulfill our contractual obligations to you
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with South African laws and regulations
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services and prevent fraud

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Sellers: To fulfill your orders
  • Service Providers: Payment processors, delivery companies, IT service providers
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@marketmyn.co.za

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure payment processing through PCI-DSS compliant providers
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Account information is retained while your account is active and for 7 years after closure for legal and accounting purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Returns & Refunds Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

MakertMyn is committed to customer satisfaction. This policy outlines your rights regarding returns and refunds in accordance with the Consumer Protection Act of South Africa.

1. Your Right to Return

You have the right to return most products within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided the product is:

  • Unused and in its original condition
  • In original packaging with all tags and labels attached
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase

2. How to Return a Product

  1. Log into your MakertMyn account
  2. Go to "My Orders" and select the order
  3. Click "Return Item" and select the reason for return
  4. Submit your return request
  5. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  6. Our courier will collect the item from your address (free collection)

3. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:

  • Approved returns are refunded within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed
  • Bank processing times may vary (typically 3-5 business days)

4. Non-Returnable Items

For health, hygiene, and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened cosmetics, skincare, and personal care products
  • Underwear, swimwear, and intimate apparel (unless unopened)
  • Perishable goods
  • Digital downloads and software
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"

5. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage/defect
  • We will arrange a free return and provide a full refund or replacement
  • You are not responsible for return shipping costs for damaged/defective items

6. Wrong Item Received

If you receive the wrong item:

  • Contact us immediately
  • We will arrange a free return
  • The correct item will be sent at no additional cost

7. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item:

  1. Return the original item for a refund
  2. Place a new order for the desired item

8. Return Shipping

  • Change of Mind Returns: Free collection within South Africa
  • Damaged/Defective/Wrong Item: Free collection and return shipping
  • International Returns: Customer responsible for return shipping costs

9. Refund Exceptions

Refunds may be denied or reduced if:

  • The item is not in its original condition
  • The item shows signs of use or wear
  • Original packaging is damaged or missing
  • Tags or labels have been removed
  • The return is made after the 30-day period

10. Seller-Specific Policies

Some sellers may have additional return policies. These will be clearly stated on the product page. Seller-specific policies do not override your statutory rights under the Consumer Protection Act.

11. Cooling-Off Period (Consumer Protection Act)

In accordance with the Consumer Protection Act, you have the right to cancel an online purchase within 7 days of receipt without providing a reason, provided the product is unused and in its original packaging.
