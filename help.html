Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or sign in, then provide your delivery address and payment details to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), as well as popular South African payment methods including PayFast, Ozow, MobiCred, Payflex, and Discovery Miles.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location:

  • Major cities (Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria): 1-3 business days
  • Other urban areas: 3-5 business days
  • Rural areas: 5-7 business days
  • Express delivery: Same day or next day (available in select areas)

Is shipping free?

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders over R500. Orders under R500 have a flat shipping fee of R60.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order in real-time through your account dashboard.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Products must be unused and in original packaging. See our Returns & Refunds Policy for full details.

Shipping Information

Delivery Areas

We deliver to all areas across South Africa, including major cities, towns, and rural locations. Some remote areas may have extended delivery times.

Delivery Partners

We work with trusted courier services including The Courier Guy, Aramex, and PostNet to ensure your orders arrive safely and on time.

Tracking Your Order

You can track your order by:

  • Logging into your Marketmyn account and viewing "My Orders"
  • Using the tracking number sent via email/SMS on the courier's website
  • Contacting our customer service team

Delivery Issues

If you experience any delivery issues, please contact us immediately at support@marketmyn.co.za or call 0800 123 456. We'll work with our courier partners to resolve the issue quickly.

Returns & Refunds

How to Return an Item

  1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the order and click "Return Item"
  3. Choose the reason for return and submit the request
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Our courier will collect the item from your address (free collection)

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened cosmetics and personal care products
  • Underwear and swimwear (unless unopened)
  • Perishable goods
  • Digital downloads
